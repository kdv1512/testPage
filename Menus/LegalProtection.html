Workplace legal protection status for the LGBT+ community

Are people who identify to be of non-conforming genders and sexualities protected by American law?

Yes. There are currently laws in the US that seek to protect employees who have non-conforming genders and sexualities from workplace discrimination and harrassment.

What kinds of workplace protection for members of the LGBT+ community are there?

Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 - which also appears in volume 42, section 2000e onwards of the United States Code - prohibits employment discrimination based on, among other things, an individual's sex.
